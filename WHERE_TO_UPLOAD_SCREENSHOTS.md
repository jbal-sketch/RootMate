# Where to Upload Screenshots in App Store Connect

## Step-by-Step Location:

### 1. Go to App Store Connect
- Visit: https://appstoreconnect.apple.com
- Sign in with your Apple Developer account

### 2. Navigate to Your App
- Click **"My Apps"** (top navigation)
- Click on **"RootMate"** (your app)

### 3. Go to Version Information
- In the left sidebar, click **"1.0 Prepare for Submission"** (or your version number)
- This is where ALL your app store listing information goes

### 4. Find the Screenshots Section
- Scroll down in the "Prepare for Submission" page
- You'll see a section called **"Screenshots"**
- It will show device sizes like:
  - **iPhone 6.7" Display** (iPhone 14 Pro Max, 15 Pro Max) â† **Start here!**
  - iPhone 6.5" Display (optional)
  - iPhone 5.5" Display (optional)

### 5. Upload Screenshots
- **Click the screenshot area** for "iPhone 6.7" Display"
- A file picker will open
- Select your screenshot files (1290 x 2796 pixels)
- Upload 3-5 screenshots
- **Drag to reorder** - first screenshot is most important!

## Quick Checklist:

- [ ] Logged into App Store Connect
- [ ] Selected "RootMate" app
- [ ] Clicked "1.0 Prepare for Submission"
- [ ] Scrolled to "Screenshots" section
- [ ] Clicked "iPhone 6.7" Display" area
- [ ] Selected screenshots from `App Store Assets/Screenshots/` folder
- [ ] Uploaded 3-5 screenshots
- [ ] Dragged to reorder (most important first)

## Tips:

1. **First screenshot matters most** - it's what users see first
2. **Drag to reorder** - put your best screenshot first
3. **Minimum 1 required** - but 3-5 is recommended
4. **Size must be 1290 x 2796** - iPhone 15 Pro Max screenshots work perfectly
5. **Format:** PNG or JPEG
